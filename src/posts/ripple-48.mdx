Wine is not something I have talked about a lot in a Ripple, but I have been an avid wine drinker for years. I have had several excellent wine experiences recently and I wanted to share. Wine is an integral part of my food enjoyment experiences and I was happy to try some great new wines.

My wife and I recently took a trip to Washington state and toured several AVAs. We visited Red Mountain, Horse Heaven Hills, and Walla Walla. Some of the best wine tasting rooms we tried were <a href="https://www.lecole.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" className="whitespace-nowrap underline decoration-neon transition hover:text-neon">L'Ecole</a>, <a href="https://kionawine.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" className="whitespace-nowrap underline decoration-neon transition hover:text-neon">Kiona Vineyards</a>, <a href="https://colsolare.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" className="whitespace-nowrap underline decoration-neon transition hover:text-neon">Col Solare</a>, <a href="https://barnardgriffin.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" className="whitespace-nowrap underline decoration-neon transition hover:text-neon">Barnyard Griffin</a>, and <a href="https://www.anelare.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" className="whitespace-nowrap underline decoration-neon transition hover:text-neon">Anelare</a>. There are a lot of great Rhone style wines, including GSM blends, coming out of this area and we enjoyed them throroughly. We also had great meals on our trip. Anelare had excellent pizzas to go with their wines, a delicious steak dinner at <a href="https://www.anthonys.com/restaurant/budds-broiler/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" className="whitespace-nowrap underline decoration-neon transition hover:text-neon">Budd's Broiler</a>, and a lovely French dinner at <a href="https://www.brasseriefour.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" className="whitespace-nowrap underline decoration-neon transition hover:text-neon">Brasserie Four</a>.

Their Monte Bello is a classic and one of my favorite wines of all time. They
also make great Zinfandels, amazing whites, though mostly Chardonnay, and other
red blends, including GSM blends. Our most recently shipment has been wonderful.
Earlier this year we took a trip to Santa Barbara and enjoyed a full weekend of
food and wine that Ridge hosted. Drinking 20 plus year old Monte Bello and
Chardonnay was wonderful.
